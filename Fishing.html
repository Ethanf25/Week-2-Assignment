Why I enjoy fishing

Fishing is a great way to relax, breathe in some fresh air, and enjoy the outdoors. It is a great way to spend time with friends and family and also a great way to catch your own food. There are many different types of fish that you can catch while fishing, my favorites to catch are Walleye, Salmon, Bass, Pike, and Panfish (Bluegill).
